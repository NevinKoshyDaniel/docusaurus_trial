About Me

Hello, I'm Nevin Koshy Daniel. I have completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering. I am a Tech‑savvy individual with experience in community engagement through student volunteering initiatives for professional societies, as well as worked as a Backend Developer in a professional capability.

As a recent graduate with a passion for computer science and technology, 
I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge to a challenging position in the industry.
I am particularly interested in roles that allow me to work on innovative projects and
collaborate with talented professionals.
